Questions

Common questions

How is this different from just using MyFitnessPal?
MyFitnessPal tracks what you eat. This tells you what to eat and why — with training-day and rest-day splits, a realistic timeline, and a coach to adjust things when life happens.
Is the Blueprint calculator really free?
Yes, completely. The full results are shown on the page. You only need to give your email if you want the PDF sent to you.
How does online coaching work?
You get a custom workout program, macro targets, and access to the JBJ client app. We check in weekly, adjust your plan as you progress, and you can message me any time.
Do I need a gym membership?
For most programs yes, but home and minimal-equipment programs are available. We work around what you have access to.
