What do these numbers mean? There are no banned foods — you can eat anything you enjoy as long as you stay within your calorie goal and hit your macro numbers. Calories are the total amount of energy in your food. Macros (protein, carbs and fats) are what those calories are made of — think of calories as the budget and macros as how you spend it. Protein and carbs each have 4 calories per gram; fat has 9. Your protein target is the most important number — it protects your muscle when cutting and is the key driver of muscle growth regardless of your goal.
